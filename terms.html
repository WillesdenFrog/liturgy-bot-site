About this service

Liturgy Bot is a private automated service that generates a weekly Children's Liturgy of the Word document for a single Catholic parish in the Diocese of Westminster. It is developed and operated by Romain Thierry. It is not a commercial product and is not available to the general public.

Intended users

This service is used by two volunteer catechists at the parish and the developer who maintains it. There are no other users. Access is not offered to external parties.

What the service does

Each Saturday, the service automatically fetches publicly available liturgical readings from diocesan websites, generates a formatted Word document with age-appropriate content for children, and emails the document to the two catechists. The catechists may edit the document before use.

Use of Google APIs

Liturgy Bot accesses a single Gmail account via the Gmail API to send outbound emails. It uses the gmail.send scope to send the weekly document and notification emails, and the gmail.readonly scope solely to verify the authenticated account identity. It does not read, search, modify, or delete any emails or other Google account data.

Liturgy Bot's use of Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
